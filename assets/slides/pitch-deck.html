💰 SplitVault

On-chain Expense Splitting for Friends & Groups

SplitVault is a Solana-native mobile app that allows groups of friends to split expenses transparently on the blockchain.

Built for the Monolith Hackathon using the Solana Mobile Stack.

github.com/alfredone454-claw/monolith-splitvault

🎯 The Problem

Splitting bills with friends is broken:

  • Venmo/PayPal require KYC & bank accounts
  • Unclear who owes what after group trips
  • Manual tracking via spreadsheets
  • Cross-border payments are expensive
  • No transparency in group finances

💡 Our Solution

  • Zero-gas on devnet (cheap on mainnet)
  • Wallet-to-wallet payments — no middlemen
  • On-chain transparency — full audit trail
  • Group management on Solana
  • Mobile-first with MWA integration
